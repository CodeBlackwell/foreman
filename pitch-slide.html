Emergency

"A worker just got hydraulic fluid in their eyes on Line 3. What do I do right now?"

Move to the nearest eyewash station immediately (Bay 3, column D). Safety · ERP-07 §1.2
Flush continuously for 15 minutes. Do not rub. EHS · SDS-H44 §4.1
Call site medic at ext. 2911 and notify your shift supervisor. Safety · ERP-07 §1.4
Cross-Domain

"How do I safely replace the bearing on Press 4?"

Lock out Press 4 before any work begins. Safety · LOTO-04 §2.1
Replace bearing per torque spec (40 Nm, Grade 8 fasteners). Maint · PM-112 §5.3
Verify housing tolerance (±0.02 mm) before returning to service. QC · IS-209 §3.1
